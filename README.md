## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is an IP video to fiber converter?  
An IP video to fiber converter extends Ethernet-based video traffic over fiber optic cable, enabling long-distance transmission for IP cameras and video devices.

### Does it support Gigabit Ethernet video streams?  
Yes — many models support 10/100/1000 Mbps speeds to accommodate high-bandwidth IP video applications.

### Can I choose fiber type and distance?  
Yes — converters are available for both single-mode and multi-mode fiber, allowing customization of transmission distance based on deployment needs.
